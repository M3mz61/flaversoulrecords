DeeAfro

Behind the Beats & the Basslines.

Welcome to the solo hub of DeeAfro. While known to many for her powerhouse collaborations and production alongside Memz, DeeAfro has been commanding dancefloors and airwaves in her own right since she first stepped behind the decks at just 18 years old. Fueled by an infectious, ever-growing passion for cutting-edge sound, she has built a reputation for delivering high-energy underground house, tech, and bass-heavy sets that keep dancefloors locked in from the first beat to the last.

The Sound & Style

DeeAfro’s signature style is a deep, energetic blend of rhythm and bass. Her solo releases and live sets seamlessly drift between heavy bass grooves, driving tech-house rhythms, and deep hypnotic club vibes. Whether commanding a dark late-night club crowd or hosting live radio shows, her passion translates into pure energy.

Key Genres: Deep House & Tech House | Bass House | Underground Club Mixes
