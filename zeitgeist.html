[LABEL_PHILOSOPHY]

Flave R Soul Records operates as a strictly independent, self-contained creative workshop. There is no commercial agenda, no external catering, and no compromise. Music is produced because it has to be—driven by lifelong internal necessity. The sole objective is the pursuit of high-quality music production and flawless mastering for personal satisfaction, backed by a deep archive of unreleased material to maintain a steady, independent output rhythm.

[CHRONOLOGICAL_HERITAGE: 2009-2017]

Technical baseline established via professional audio engineering credentials at Alchemea College alongside classmate Lorenzo Panico. Following academy graduation, executed comprehensive engineering solutions and mastering for the entire legacy Polluted Records catalog across both Physical Vinyl pressings and Digital Download retail avenues spanning 2009–2013. Expanded operational focus into intensive Deep House, Nu Disco, and Tech House tracking, composition, and performance windows from 2012 through 2017.

[UNCREDITED_PRODUCTION_MANIFEST: ARCHIVE_REF_F4U]

Historical record of tracking, arrangement, and original mix engineering for the composition profile historically indexed as "Fallin' 4U". All foundational instrumentation, baseline arrangement architecture, and final master bus compression were executed entirely within this workshop's studio environment. Following the expiration of initial distribution agreements, master rights and multi-format commercial pressings were reassigned externally without authorization or compensatory settlement. The acoustic blueprint remains the exclusive creative product of this facility's engineer.

[STUDIO_MATRIX_UPGRADES]

The monitoring and conversion infrastructure has been systematically migrated away from legacy legacy frameworks (Digi002r coupled with a Focusrite Scarlett Octopre Dynamic via ADAT optical expansion) to a high-density DSP processing matrix. Current tracking relies on an Universal Audio Apollo Quad matched with a UAD Octo Core PCIe/Satellite array, supplying a total footprint of 12 SHARC hardware processors dedicated solely to running uncompromised UAD mastering plug-in algorithms.

[HYBRID_OUTBOARD_HARDWARE]

Digital processing blocks are summed through a custom outboard hardware rack profile for analog saturation and character tracking. Dynamic hardware processors include a Behringer Tube Composer utilized for warm tube-stage bus leveling alongside a vintage Korg M3R synthesis module handling multi-timbral MIDI sound generation directly into the analog console inputs.

[CORE_INSTRUMENTATION]

Live instrumentation serves as the foundation for 90% of all tracking sessions. Elite bass and guitar passes are performed natively. The studio rhythm section utilizes a hybrid setup: tracking acoustic percussion elements via an alesis DM5 drum module triggering an upgraded, mesh-skin Roland snare pad for realistic transient response, alongside a Roland TR-8S rhythm performer handling dedicated EDM drum synthesis. Hardware includes a Gibson J-45, a 1950s Heritage Reissue Les Paul Standard, and a Yamaha MX88 workstation.

[AMPLIFICATION_SIGNAL_CHAIN]

High-voltage guitar capture is routed through a pure analog hardware back-line. The primary tracking matrix utilizes a vintage 1979 Marshall JMP Super Lead 100W head and a 1990 Marshall JCM900 Dual Channel amplifier. Heavy power amplification curves are pushed directly into a matching heavy-duty 1979 Marshall 1960B straight cabinet to preserve authentic vintage air movement and harmonic breakup at the capsule.
